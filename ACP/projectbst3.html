My list of hobbies

Journalling is one of my substitues for therapy and is one of my most awaited activites of the week. Journalling is my art of expression and resonates with me deeply. Give it a shot!

Writing letters

Okay, this HAS to be my most beloved hobby EVER! I absolutely love writing letters to my friends and family. I'm the type of person who appreciates heartfelt, personal gifts rather than materialistic gifts. So, OBVIOUSLY I give letters ALL THE TIME!!

Painting

This is one of the rarer hobbies i have. I don't usually paint, because it feels like a lot of work finding the perfect brushes AND the ideas. I'm a creative artist, i only paint when i have buckets of ideas, otherwise painting is DEAD to me.
