About Codingal

A little bit about me

Codingal is on a mission to inspire kids to fall in love with coding. Coding helps develop logical thinking skills and problem-solving skills. Coding jobs are the future. They already constitute more than 60% of all the jobs in STEM. While still in school, those who start young will be ahead of everyone by the time they get into college. They will be creators of the future.

In today's age, coding has become an essential skill. Coding for kids helps them interact with technology better. Studies also show that it aids in holistic developement of the child, improving cognitive functioning as well as logical thinking. Holistic development means evolving cognitive, physical, and social skills of a child to prepare them better for the interactions with the world. Coding as an activity not only involved mental development but also focuses on social understanding.
